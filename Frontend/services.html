Services

We write appraisals for Insurance, Fair Market, Donation, Estate Planning, Estate Tax, Probate and Inventory Services. Our clients include private collectors, museums, banks, attorneys, insurance providers, accountants, art galleries, auctioneers and trustees.

Fees

Appraisal services are billed at $225.00 per hour per appraiser. There is no additional charge for travel within a 2 hour radius of the Los Angeles area. All fees and additional costs must be agreed upon prior to the appointment.

The Appraisal Process

Depending upon the type of evaluation required our services normally include physically inspecting, writing descriptions, photographing and measuring each piece to be appraised. The next step is to do all the research to determine the value of each object. The final step is to write the report for presentation to our clients.

Each of our appraisers is USPAP accredited. All appraisals are written according to USPAP (Uniform Standard of Professional Appraisal Practice) standards.
